Comprar USDC pra sua carteira Nexus Inovation

Nexus Inovation wallets accept deposits in USDC on the Base L2 network. This guide walks through the three common paths to get USDC on Base, ranked by speed and fees. All options are third-party providers; Nexus Inovation is not involved in the fiat-to-crypto step (see our Terms, Section 11).

⚠️ Before you send anything

Only send USDC — not USDT, not ETH, not BRL. Other tokens are unrecoverable.

Only use the Base network — not Ethereum mainnet, Polygon, Arbitrum, or Optimism. Wrong network = permanent loss.

Double-check your Nexus Inovation deposit address in your dashboard before confirming the withdrawal.

1. Brazil — Bitso (recommended for BRL holders)

Recommended BR Bitso

Fee: ~1.5-2% spread + small withdrawal fee KYC: ~30 min with CPF Timing: minutes

Bitso is a Latin-American exchange that supports BRL deposits via PIX, sells USDC natively, and — crucially — withdraws USDC directly on Base (no bridge needed, no Ethereum gas fees).

Step by step

  1. Sign up at bitso.com. Choose Brazil.
  2. Complete KYC: CPF + selfie with document. Typically approved in 10-30 minutes.
  3. Deposit BRL via PIX (instant, free). Copy the Bitso PIX key, pay from your bank app.
  4. Go to the USDC market. Place a buy order (market or limit).
  5. Go to Withdraw → Crypto → USDC. When prompted for the network, choose Base.
  6. Paste your Nexus Inovation deposit address (copy it from /dashboard).
  7. Confirm. The deposit will appear in your Nexus Inovation balance within ~2 minutes of on-chain confirmation.

What it costs

Bitso charges a spread on the USDC purchase (typically 1-2%) and a small fixed withdrawal fee (currently about $0.10-$0.50 USDC). For a $20 deposit, total cost is usually under $1.

2. International — Coinbase, Kraken

These exchanges support Base-native USDC withdrawals and are well- regulated, though with more friction than Bitso for Brazilian users.

Well-supported Coinbase

Fee: 1.49% + network fee KYC: 1-3 days Timing: minutes after approval

Coinbase operates Base, so their USDC-on-Base support is the most robust of any exchange. Best option for US and most EU users.

  1. Sign up at coinbase.com.
  2. Complete identity verification (driver's license or passport).
  3. Fund via bank transfer (ACH free in US, SEPA in EU) or debit card (1.49% fee).
  4. Buy USDC.
  5. Go to Send → Crypto. Select USDC, network Base, paste your Nexus Inovation deposit address.

Solid alternative Kraken

Fee: 0.16-0.26% (Pro interface) KYC: 1-2 days Timing: minutes

Cheaper than Coinbase for larger amounts. Supports Base USDC withdrawals. Not available in all US states.

  1. Sign up at kraken.com, complete KYC.
  2. Deposit USD, EUR, or similar via wire.
  3. Buy USDC in the Pro interface (lower fees than instant-buy).
  4. Withdraw USDC on the Base network to your Nexus Inovation address.

3. Card purchase — MoonPay, Ramp

No full KYC, no exchange account, just a debit/credit card. You pay the convenience with higher fees (typically 3-5%) and lower purchase limits.

Card direct MoonPay

Fee: ~3.5-4.5% KYC: light (email + card) Timing: minutes

MoonPay lets you buy crypto directly with a debit or credit card and withdraw to any wallet. Select Base when choosing the network.

  1. Go to moonpay.com/buy/usdc.
  2. Enter amount, choose network Base, paste your Nexus Inovation deposit address.
  3. Pay with card. USDC arrives in your wallet within minutes.

Card direct Ramp

Fee: ~2.9-3.9% KYC: light Timing: minutes

Ramp is a MoonPay competitor with slightly lower fees and clean UX. Also supports Base-native USDC.

  1. Go to ramp.network.
  2. Select USDC, network Base, enter your Nexus Inovation address.
  3. Pay with card. Expect 1-5 minutes for confirmation.

Pro tips

  • Test with a small amount first. If this is your first on-chain withdrawal, send $2-5 as a test before committing larger amounts. The Nexus Inovation free tier includes $0.50 of credit — plenty to verify your account works.
  • Save your deposit address. Your Nexus Inovation wallet address is fixed; you can reuse it for every future deposit. Add it to your exchange's address book so you don't re-type it.
  • Gas fees on Base are negligible. A typical USDC transfer on Base costs about 0.1 cents. Don't let legacy-Ethereum fee anxiety keep you from depositing.
  • Fiat → USDC conversion is your own tax event. In most jurisdictions, buying USDC is not taxable, but capital gains may apply if you sell it back. Consult your accountant. Nexus Inovation does not provide tax advice.

FAQ

How long until my deposit is credited?

Base has ~2-second block times. Once the transfer has 1 confirmation, our webhook credits your balance — typically under 2 minutes total from the time you hit send. If it takes longer than 1 hour, contact us.

What happens if I send the wrong token or network?

It's gone. We have no way to recover tokens sent to the wrong chain or the wrong asset. Always double-check before confirming the withdrawal.

Can I deposit from a DEX or a bridge?

Yes. If you already hold USDC on Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism, or any chain with a bridge to Base, bridge first then send to your Nexus Inovation address. Base's native bridge is at bridge.base.org. Third-party aggregators (Across, Li.Fi, etc.) are often faster.

Is there a minimum deposit?

No Nexus Inovation-side minimum, but exchange withdrawal minimums typically apply (Bitso: $1-2 USDC, Coinbase: similar). Below ~$2 the withdrawal fee becomes a significant percentage.

Can I withdraw USDC back out?

Balance withdrawal is being rolled out; current status is reflected in the status page and the repository README. In the meantime, unused balance stays in your account indefinitely.

Do you earn a commission from these providers?

Not yet. When we activate affiliate relationships with any of the above, we'll mark those links clearly. Our current recommendation above is based on feature coverage (Base-native USDC support) and fee comparisons, not commission.
