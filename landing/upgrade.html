FAQ

How does USDC subscription work?

When you subscribe, the plan price is debited once from your Axon wallet. Your tier activates for 30 days. Auto-renew (on by default) charges the same wallet on day 30 — if there's enough balance, you stay subscribed; otherwise you fall back to free and we email you so you can top up.

Can I cancel?

Yes — anytime. Cancellation just disables auto-renew. You keep your paid tier (rate limits, markup discount, all benefits) until the end of the period you already paid for, then drop to free. No partial refunds — the period was already prepaid.

What if my wallet balance is too low?

If you try to subscribe with less than the plan price, the call returns a 402 with insufficient-funds. Buy USDC first. If your auto-renew triggers with insufficient balance, your tier drops to free and we email you — top up and re-subscribe.

Does the markup discount apply to free upstreams too?

Yes. Even on free upstreams (Open-Meteo, Wikipedia, BrasilAPI, etc.) we apply a small Axon markup to cover infrastructure. Pro/Team users get that markup discounted by 25% / 50% across the entire catalog.

Why USDC and not credit card?

Axon is built on x402 + USDC for a reason: zero processing fees, instant settlement, no chargebacks, no fiat conversion. Subscriptions reusing the same wallet keeps the model coherent — your USDC funds API calls AND your plan, no separate billing relationship.

Can I switch between Pro and Team mid-period?

Yes. Subscribing to Team while on Pro extends from your current expiry rather than resetting it — you don't lose paid days. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal.
