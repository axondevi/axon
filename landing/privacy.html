Privacy

Last updated: 2026-04-22

Axon is early-stage. This is the honest short version of what we do with your data. If anything here is unclear, email shopkaolin.com@gmail.com.

What we collect

  • Email (when you request access or sign up)
  • An API key we issue you (hashed in our DB; plaintext only shown once)
  • Your request history: which API you called, when, how much it cost, whether it hit cache
  • Your wallet balance and transactions
  • Standard server logs (IP, user agent) for debugging, kept ≤30 days

What we don't collect

  • The contents of your API requests or responses (we proxy them, we don't store them beyond cache TTL)
  • Any personal info beyond email
  • Tracking cookies, ad IDs, or analytics that identify you across sites

Who sees your data

  • Us (the Axon team — one person currently)
  • Upstream API providers (OpenAI, Jina, SerpAPI, etc.) — they see whatever you send them through our proxy
  • Infrastructure (Render, Neon, Upstash, Cloudflare) — they host our servers and database
  • Nobody else. We don't sell data. We don't run ads.

Data retention

  • Request metadata: kept indefinitely for usage/billing. You can email us to delete.
  • Server logs: ≤30 days
  • Cache entries: per-API TTL (see stats)

On-chain data

Deposits to your Axon wallet are recorded on Base (public blockchain). Anyone can see transaction amounts and timestamps via a block explorer. We don't link your wallet address to your email publicly.

Your rights

Email us to: delete your account, export your data, opt out of communications. We respond within 72 hours.
