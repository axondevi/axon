1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how personal data are collected, used, shared, and protected when you use the Nexus Inovation service, including the websites at axon-5zf.pages.dev and api.nexusinovation.com.br and associated APIs (collectively, the "Service"). This Policy should be read together with our Terms of Service.

2. Controller

The data controller is the natural person operating Nexus Inovation under the trade name "Nexus Inovation", domiciled in Brazil (the "Operator"). All privacy inquiries, subject-access requests, and data-deletion requests should be directed to shopkaolin.com@gmail.com.

3. Data We Collect

  • Account data — the email address you provide at signup and a hashed representation of your API key.
  • Transaction metadata — per-request records containing: the Upstream Provider and endpoint called, timestamp, latency, cost in USDC, cache-hit status, and response status code. We do not retain the content of requests or responses beyond cache time-to-live (see Section 9).
  • Wallet data — the on-chain address generated for you on the Base L2 network and associated balance records.
  • Technical logs — standard server logs containing IP address, user-agent string, and request path, retained for operational debugging for no longer than thirty (30) days.
  • Communications — messages you send to us and any attachments.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

  • The body of your API requests or responses, beyond what is retained in cache under a cryptographic hash key as described in Section 9;
  • Payment-card information or fiat bank-account details (we do not handle fiat);
  • Third-party advertising identifiers or cross-site tracking data;
  • Biometric data, precise geolocation, or any special category of personal data as defined under applicable law.

5. Purposes and Legal Bases for Processing

We process the categories in Section 3 to:

  • Provide, maintain, and secure the Service and authenticate your requests (legal basis: performance of contract);
  • Calculate billing and maintain accurate balance records (contract);
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate abuse, fraud, and security incidents (legitimate interests; legal obligation);
  • Comply with applicable law, including Brazilian Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD, Law 13,709/2018), and respond to lawful requests from authorities (legal obligation);
  • Communicate operational changes, incidents, and legal updates affecting your use of the Service (legitimate interests).

6. Sharing and Sub-Processors

We do not sell personal data and we do not share it except as described below:

  • Upstream Providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Jina, SerpAPI) receive whatever data you elect to transmit through the Service to their endpoints; their terms and privacy policies govern their further processing.
  • Infrastructure sub-processors. We engage the following providers to host and operate the Service:
    • Render, Inc. (application hosting);
    • Neon Inc. (managed PostgreSQL);
    • Upstash, Inc. (managed Redis);
    • Cloudflare, Inc. (static content delivery and DNS);
    • Turnkey Platform, Inc. (key-management infrastructure for wallet creation).
  • Legal compliance. We may disclose data where required to comply with a valid legal process, to enforce these Terms, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of any person.
  • Business transfer. In the event of merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets, User data may be transferred to the successor entity under equivalent protections.

7. On-Chain Data

Deposits, withdrawals, and settlement transactions conducted on the Base L2 network are, by the nature of public blockchains, permanently recorded and publicly observable via block explorers. While the Service does not publicly associate wallet addresses with email addresses, any counterparty or observer who knows your wallet address can view its on-chain activity.

8. International Transfers

The sub-processors listed in Section 6 operate data centers outside Brazil, including in the United States and the European Union. Where such transfers occur, they are performed under adequate safeguards as required by applicable data-protection law, including LGPD Article 33 (adequacy, standard contractual clauses, or specific consent) and, where relevant, Article 46 of the EU GDPR.

9. Retention

  • Transaction metadata: retained for the life of the account and, after account closure, for up to five (5) years for accounting and legal-compliance purposes, unless earlier deletion is requested and legally permissible.
  • Technical logs: up to thirty (30) days.
  • Cache entries: per-endpoint time-to-live, as published in the public stats endpoint and documentation.
  • Account email and hashed API key: retained until account closure plus any legally required retention period.

10. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include transport encryption (TLS 1.2+), at-rest encryption of credential material, isolated key-management via the Turnkey infrastructure, hashing of API keys, access controls, and audit logging. No system can be guaranteed to be fully secure; in the event of a data breach we will notify affected Users and competent authorities where required by law.

11. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law (LGPD in Brazil, GDPR in the European Economic Area, and equivalent frameworks elsewhere), you may have the right to:

  • confirm that we process data concerning you, and access that data;
  • rectify inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • request deletion (subject to retention obligations);
  • request portability of data you provided;
  • restrict or object to certain processing;
  • withdraw consent where processing is based on consent, without affecting prior lawful processing;
  • lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority — in Brazil, the Autoridade Nacional de Proteção de Dados (ANPD).

To exercise any of these rights, contact shopkaolin.com@gmail.com. We will respond within the period required by applicable law (typically fifteen (15) days under LGPD, thirty (30) days under GDPR).

12. Children

The Service is not directed to individuals under eighteen (18) years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from such individuals. If you believe a minor has provided data to us, contact us and we will delete it.

13. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Service's websites use only strictly necessary storage, such as browser localStorage to cache your API key for dashboard convenience and standard session cookies set by our infrastructure providers. We do not set advertising, analytics, or cross-site tracking cookies.

14. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Updated versions take effect on their publication date, reflected in the "Effective date" above. Material changes affecting your rights will be announced by email to registered Users at least fifteen (15) days before taking effect. Your continued use of the Service after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

15. Contact

All privacy inquiries, requests to exercise data-subject rights, and incident notifications: shopkaolin.com@gmail.com. See also our Terms of Service.
